8.

(+) the mouse is caught by tiger into jungle (-) the mouse is not caught by tiger into jungle (?) is the mouse caught by tiger into jungle ?

9.

(+) a plate of fried rice will be made by her (-) A plate of fried rice will not be made by her (?) will A plate of fried rice be made by her ?

10.

(+) her motorcycle is being repaired by tom right now (-) her motorcycle is not being repaired by tom right now (?) is her motorcycle being repaired by tom right now ?

PEMBAHASANA. Present Tense

No. 8 menggunakan Passive dari Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By

(-) S + Be (is/am/are) + not +V3 + By

(?) Be (is/am/are) + S + V3 + By?

B. Future Tense

No. 9 menggunakan Passive dari Rumus Future Tense

(+) S + will + Be + V3 + By

(-) S + will +not + Be + V3 + By

(?) will + S + Be + V3 + By?

C. Present Continuous

No. 10 menggunakan Passive dari Rumus Present Continuous

(+) S + is/am/are + being + V3 + By

(-) S + is/am/are + not + being + V3 + By

(?) is/am/are + S + being + V3 + By ?

Kalimat conditional berupa kalimat yang mencirikan sesuatu yang tidak berdasarkan fakta, melainkan fakta berbeda dengan kondisi yang ada.

Penggunaan:

S + would + V1 + ROS + if + O + V2 + ROSIn fact + S + will + not + V1 + ROS + because + O + don't/doesn't + V1 + ROS

ROS = rest of sentence (O + C)

Contohnya:

I would go if you accompanied me, in fact I will not go because you don't accompany me

I would go hanya suatu kata yang diharapkan agar semoga obyek dapat menemani subyek pergi tapi pada kenyataan subyek tidak akan pergi karena obyek tidak menemaninya

Kesimpulan:

Conditional sentence merupakan kalimat yang mencirikan seseorang terhadap sesuatu yang bukan berdasarkan fakta. Sesuatu yang diambakan harap dapat terjadi pada kenyataan. Tapi, itu semua berdasarkan kondisi/keadaan yang ada dimana subyek belum tentu bisa mendapatkan/melakukan apa yang ada di pikirannya
