Penjelasan:

karena kamu diminta untuk mengisi menggunakan possesive adjectives maka kamu bisa gunakan my, your, our, their, his, her, its.

possesive adjectives digunakan untuk menyatakan kepemilikan terhadap kata benda (noun).

contoh : i am looking for my pen. (pen : kata benda)

: is that your house? (house : kata benda)

untuk nomor 5, sepertinya harus diisi menggunakan subject pronouns ( i, you, we , they, he , she, it)

subject pronouns possesive adjectives

i = my

you = your

we = our

they = their

he = his

she = her

it = its

17. penyelesaian tugas bahasa inggris,halaman 89,kelas 8


Describing Animals

2. Tigers

Habitat: Grasslands and forests.Food: They love meat.Activity: They huntCharacteristic: They are the biggest of all cats. They have stripped coat that gives them good camouflage when they hunt.

3. Bears

Habitat: They live in northern parts of the world.Food: Some of the bears eat meat, and some of them eat honey.Activity: They find snug places to hibernate in during the winter.Characteristic: Bears have thick fur coats to protect them from the cold. They are large and powerful, they also have a good sense of smell.

4. Monkeys

Habitat: They live in the forest.Food: They eat plants, birds’ eggs, small animals and insects.Activity: They hold things with their hands.Characteristic: They are clever animals and can solve problems, they also live in groups that we call a ’troop’.

Pembahasan

Untuk mengidentifikasi binatang biasanya kita menggunakan beberapa informasi secara umum seperti activity, habitat, food dan characteristic. Berikut adalah penjelasannya.

Activity is the condition in which things are happening or being done. Jadi, activity pada dasarnya adalah kegiatan yang dilakukan oleh binatang tersebut.

Habitat is the natural home or environment of an animal, plant or other organism. Sehingga habitat adalah suatu tempat atau lokasi alami dari suatu binatang, tumbuhan atau organisme.

Food is any nutritious substance that people or animals eat or drink or that plants absorbs in order to maintain life and growth. Sederhananya, food adalah makanan yang dikonsumsi oleh manusia, binatang, atau yang diserap tumbuhan agar dapat bertumbuh dan bertahan hidup.

Characteristic is a typical or noticeable quality of someone or something. Karakteristik merupakan ciri khas yang jelas tentang sesuatu atau seseorang. Sehingga untuk identifikasi binatang, yang dilihat adalah ciri-ciri fisik dari binatang.
