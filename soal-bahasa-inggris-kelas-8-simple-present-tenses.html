4. Pengertian present tenses continuous dalam bahasa Inggris.


Jawaban:

Present continuouse tense is used when we want to describe something that started in the past but still in progress at present time or activity that by now has not yet finished or completed.

Penjelasan:

Present continuous tense digunakan ketika kita ingin menggambarkan sesuatu yang dimulai di masa lalu tetapi masih berlangsung pada saat ini atau kegiatan yang sekarang belum selesai atau tuntas.

Jadi ciri-ciri kalimat dalam present continuous tense adalah bahwa pada saat ini (yaitu pada saat kalimat itu diucapkan), kegiatan itu belum kelar, belum selesai, masih berjalan atau masih berlangsung.

5. Tenses simple present


Tenses simple present
(+) s + v1 s/es + o
(-) s + not + v1 + o
(?) do/does + s + v1 + o +?

Do : I, u, they, we
Does : he, she, it.

Untuk org ke3 tunggal(He, she, it) pada kalimat + verbnya tambahkan s/es. simple present tense digunakan utk kalimat yg menunjukkan suatu kegiatan yg mjd kebiasaan atau selalu berulang.
rumus nominal simple present tense: s+ am/is/are+ noun.
contoh: i am student. my father is a farmer. she is a diligent student.

rumus verbal simple present tense:
1. s (i, you, they, we) +verb
contoh: i read this book every day.
they play in the school yard every break time.

2. s (she, he, it) + verb with s/es
contoh:
she reads this book every day.
the dog catches the cat.

Simple present tenses dalam teks

1. About elephants

Elephants are the heaviest land animals.They are also intelligent and have good memories.Most of them live in Afrika and Asia.They use in Lampung, Indonesia.They use their long trunks almost like an arm.They eat grass and plants.

2. About tigers

Tigers are the biggest of all cats.They live in grasslands and forests.Their stripped coat gives them good camouflage when they hunt.They love meat to eat.

3. About bears

Bear have thick four coats ...Most of them live in northern parts of the world. They are large and powerful. They have a good sense of smell. Some of them eat meat and some of them eat honey. Some bears find a snug place to hibernate.

4. About monkeys

Monkeys are clever mammals ...They live in groups called troops. Monkey eats plants, birds, eggs, small animals, and insects.Most of them live in the forest.

Simple present tense merupakan tenses sederhana yang menandakan peristiwa dilakukan waktu sekarang atau kenyataan.

Pembahasan

Simple Present Tense biasanya digunakan untuk menyatakan kebiasaan, kebenaran umum, rutinitas, kenyataan, atau pendapat.

Penanda waktu yang digunakan: often, usually, always, sometimes, rarely, seldom, every day, twice a week, nowadays.

Bentuk simple present terbagi menjadi dua, yaitu nominal sentence dan verbal sentence.

Pola nominal sentence: S + is/am/are + OPola verbal sentence: S + verb base or + s/s + O

Example:

She is a doctor. (nominal sentence)Water boils at 100 degrees Celsius.

3). We practice dancing regularly.

Answer :

Answer :

5). The barbershop closes at 5 o'clock p.m

Answer :

Does the barbershop close at 5 o'clcok pm?

Penjelasan:

Untuk membuat kalimat interrogative dari simple present, maka rumus yang digunakan adalah :

Do/does + S + V1 + O

Catatan :

DO digunakan untuk subject I/you/they/we/plural subject.DOES digunakan untuk subject kata ganti orang ketiga tunggal seperti she/he/it

Tulislah pertanyaan dalam simple present!

6). Where does your father work?

Karena subject nya orang ketiga tunggal "your father" maka menggunakan "does".

7). Why do we learn English?

Karena subject nya "we" maka menggunakan auxiliary verb "do".

8). How do I turn on the computer?

Karena subject nya "I" maka menggunakan auxiliary verb "do".

Catatan :

Untuk membuat kalimat tanya menggunakan wh-question rumus nya tidak jauh berbeda dengan rumus interrogative simple present di atas.Hanya saja karena ada wh-question (where/why/how/etc), maka harus diletakkan di depan sebelum auxiliary verb do/does.

Rumus simple present tense yaitu

(+) subject + verb 1

(-) Subject + do/does + not + verb 1

(?) Do/does + subject + verb 1?

Rumus simple past tense yaitu

(+) subject + verb 2

(-) Subject + did + not + verb 1

(?) Did + subject + verb 1?

Rumus simple future tense yaitu

(+) subject + will + verb 1

(-) Subject + will + not + verb 1

(?) Will+ subject + verb 1?

(+) I slept on the sofa last night.

(-) I didn't sleep on the sofa last night.

(?) Did I sleep on the sofa last night?

Contoh kalimat simple future tense yaitu

(+) She will come to my house tomorrow.

(-) She will not come to my house tomorrow.

(?) Will she come to my house tomorrow?
