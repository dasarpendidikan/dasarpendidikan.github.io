Dean: my ex broke up again?

Sam: noooo... remember the dream car that you always wanted?

Dean: yeah, the Chevrolet Impala 1967 black color, just like in the movie supernatural.

Sam: yes, i have just checked the price 2 hours ago, and the price went downhill, what do you say?

Dean: no wayy... thanks for the good news Sammy, i almost bought a Fiat if that car is still expensive

Sam: what do you say? visit the car shop?

Dean: let's waste no time!

Sam: oh and one more good news...

Dean: is it about the car again? if it is i will be very happy

Sam: well... no, it's about me. my resume is accepted at the coca-cola company

Dean: shut up! no way dude, congrats

Sam: and the best part is, i got to be the manager right away, because the last one is fired

Dean: dude... we should have a beer after we buy my dream car!

Sam: yes, we probably can, but can we make it before 5 p.m? i got a date with jessica

Dean: sure, we'll make it quick, pack your stuff, and get ready to the train station

Sam: got it Dean, and who will pay the train?

Dean: you will, i paid last time, remember?

Sam: wait, but last time was... alright, i'll pay

Dean: come on, it's the last time we have to pay for public transpoertation, after this, we will be back with my impala

Sam: okay Dean
