I Eat NoodleSimple Present Tense

Simple present tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan habit (kebiasaan), unchanging situations, general truths dan fixed arrangement.

Simple present tense dibentuk dengan menggunakan verb-1 seperti like, decide, love, eat, walk. Tetapi untuk orang ketiga tunggal (He, she, my mother, Rani, Adit, Cat) dilakukan dengan menambahkan -s pada verb-1.

Untuk membentuk kalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan DOES (= the third person of the auxiliary 'DO') + infinitive of the verb. Untuk lebih jelasnya mari kita lihat contoh kalimat berikut ini :

(+) He walks to school every day.

(-) He doesn't walk to school every day.

(?) Does he walk to school every day?

(+) They want to finish their homework.

(-) They don't want to finish their homework.

(?) Do they want to finish their homework?

(+) Penguins live in the Antarctic.

(-) Penguins do not live in the Antarctic.

(?) Do penguins live in the Antarctic?

Dilihat dari contoh diatas, maka dapat disimpulkan bahwa :

ketika subyeknya tunggal : He, she verb yang digunakan dengan menambahkan-s dan kalimat negatif dan interrogative-nya menggunakan "DOES' + verb-1 (- akhiran s pada verb hilang)Untuk kalimat yang subyeknya jamak/plural seperti they, penguins, the students, maka verb yang digunakan adalah verb-1 without -s. Dan kalimat negatif dan interrogative-nya menggunakan 'DO'Keterangan waktu yang biasa mengikut simple present tense diantaranya: every day, always, regularly, daily, normally, generally, usually, occasionally, sometimes, often, rarely, frequently, nowadays, naturally, constantly, never, every year, once a year.Pembahasan

27. when do we use V1/V1+s/es ??


we use v1/v1+s/es if we are talking about present tense 
v1 =for subject i,you ,they ,we
v1+s/es=for subject he,she ,it.
when you use simple present tense.

Pattern of simple present tense :

Subject + auxilary + V1

Example for V1 :

I go to school by bike --> we use 'go' because the auxilary of I is 'do'

I, you, they, and we use 'do' for auxilary

Example for V1es :

She goes to school by bike--> we use 'goes' because the auxilary of She is 'does'

He, she, it, use does for auxilary.

Simple Present Tense:

Kalimat Positive (+)  :

Verbal     :  Subject + Verb1/Verb1+s/es + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Negatif (-)    :

Verbal     :  Subject + do/does not +Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Interrogative (?)  :

Verbal    :  Do/Does + Subject + Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning) + ?
