3. Boarding: Masuk ke dalam pesawat

4. Passport: Paspor, Visa: Visa

7. retaurant: restoran cafe: kafe

8. tour agent: agen tur

9. train station: stasiun kereta api

10. Ship: Kapal laut

11. Boat: Perahu

12. Port (U.S.) / Harbor (U.K): Pelabuhan

4. contoh experience the during holidays


Translate : Pengalam saat liburan


Contoh pengalaman :


New Year Holiday Story


The last school holiday, I spent the time with my family on vacation to a relative's house in Madiun, approximately 6 hour trip by bus from our home in Malang.


On the way there, we saw many beautiful views such as rice fields, mountains, etc. Having arrived at the uncle's house, we were greeted by our relatives who had not met us for a long time. The next day, we walked around the town of Madiun until the sky got dark. Finally we decided to go home at 6:00 p.m. on that day.


On the next day, I forgot that it was the New Year. Sudenly, one of my cousins ​​came and asked me to celebrate the New Year's Eve together. At night, I went together with him to the center of the city of Madiun which had already been so crowded because a lot of people who also wanted to celebrate the New Year with his family. When the countdown arrived, the sky which was dark at once then became so colorfully decorated by fireworks. We prayed together and watched the fireworks lightening the sky that night.


Suddenly, the holiday passed quickly. The last New Year's holiday experience in the city of Malang was so impressive for me.

7. contoh speech tentang holidays


Jawaban:

Good morning, friends. I would like to present my holiday with my friends.

Do you know Bali?. My friends and I decided to visit Bali on our school holiday last month. There were a lot of places to see in there. Because we never went to Bali before, we joined the tours to see as much as possible. My friends and I stayed in one of hotel in Kuta on arrival. ln Kuta beach, we spent our two days by surfing and swimming. Fikri, one of my friends tried to sunbathe as if he were a foreigner.

We really enjoyed our time there. On the next day, we were ready to visit the other place listed by the tour guide. the first place we visited was Singaraja. Singaraja is a city of about 95 thousands people. lt is a busy city but quiet town. The street are lined with trees adn ther are a lot of old Dutch houses. We learnt about history of Singaraja there. Then we returned very late to the hotel. We went to bed to regain our stamina.

Our last tour destination was Ubud. There were a lot of art and craft in Ubud. The first stop was at Batu bulan, a center of stone sclupture. We watched young boys were carving away at big block of stone. The next place we visited was Celuk, a center for silversmiths and golden smiths. After that, we had a lunch at Sukawati and continued touring to Mass, a tourist center. We bought some souvenirs in there. Our holiday ended very quickly.

Beside visiting some places, all our days were spent on the beach. We went sailing or surfboarding every day. We really enjoyed our holiday in Bali. Thank you.

Penjelasan:

Artinya :

Selamat pagi teman-teman disini saya akan mempresentasikan tugas saya saat liburan. Saya dan teman-teman memutuskan untuk mengunjungi Bali pada liburan sekolah kami bulan lalu. Ada banyak tempat untuk dikunjungi di sana. Karena kita tidak pernah pergi ke Bali sebelumnya, kita bergabung dengan tur guide untuk mengunjungi tempat wisata sebanyak mungkin.

Kami tinggal di salah satu hotel di Kuta. Di Pantai Kuta, kami menghabiskan hari-hari kami dengan berselancar dan berenang. Fikri, salah satu teman saya mencoba untuk berjemur seolah-olah ia turis mancanegara.

Kami benar-benar menikmati waktu kami di sana. Pada keesokan harinya, kami sudah siap untuk mengunjungi tempat lain yang sudah terjadwal oleh pemandu wisata. tempat pertama yang dikunjungi adalah Singaraja. Singaraja adalah sebuah kota sekitar 95 ribuan penduduk.

Singaraja adalah kota yang sibuk tetapi kota sangat tenang. Jalan berjajar dengan pohon-pohon dan ada banyak sekali rumah tua peninggalan Belanda. Kita belajar tentang sejarah Singaraja di sana. Kemudian kami kembali ke hotel larut malam. Kami pergi ke tempat tidur untuk memulihkan stamina kami.

Tujuan wisata kami terakhir adalah Ubud. Ada banyak karya seni dan kerajinan di Ubud. Pemberhentian pertama pada Batu bulan, Pusat patung batu. Kami melihat anak laki-laki yang sedang mengukir pada batu ukuran sangat besar. Tempat berikutnya kami mengunjungi adalah Celuk, Pusat pengrajin perak dan emas.

Setelah itu, kami telah makan siang di Sukawati dan melanjutkan tur ke Mass, pusat pariwisata. Kami membeli beberapa souvenir di sana. Liburan kami berakhir dengan sangat cepat. Selain mengunjungi beberapa tempat, hari-hari kami banyak dihabiskan di pantai. Kami pergi berlayar atau menyelam setiap hari. Kami benar-benar menikmati liburan kami di Bali. Terima kasih.
