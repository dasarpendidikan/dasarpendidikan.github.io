5. jelaskan dan bedakan antara manives sosial problem dan laten sosial problem beserta contoh​


Jawaban:

-manifest social problem merupakan fenomena sosial yg dirasakan dan dianggap masalah oleh masyarakat. contoh: kemacetan yg terjadi di jakarta. kemacetan ini dianggap masalah dan masyarakat berusaha mencari jalan keluarnya.

-latent social problem merupakan fenomena yang dirasakan oleh masyarakat dan masyarakat tau kalau itu berdampak terhadap masyarakat tetapi tidak dianggap masalah oleh sebagian besar masyarakat. contoh: dampak kemacetan adalah polusi udara. masyarakat tau kalau polusi udara berbahaya bagi kesehatan, tetapi tidak terlalu dipermasalahkan oleh masyarakat

31. Urutan bahasa Inggris dari As see lack funding the it is problem far i the of as


Jumbled words adalah kata - kata acak yang nantinya diurutkan menjadi kalimat padu.

As - see - lack - funding - the - it - is - problem - far - i - the - of - as

menjadi

As far as I see, the problem is the lack of funding it Pembahasan

Cara menyusun kata - kata acak menjadi kalimat padu adalah:

Baca dan perhatikan kata per kata, apakah kalimatnya berbentuk affirmative, negative, atau interrogative.Temukan subyeknyaJika tidak ditemukan subyeknya, carilah verbnya.Kemudian carilah obyeknyaCarilah adverbnyaSusunlah menjadi kalimat padu dan bermakna

Contoh:

Bought - car - a - I - yesterday -new --> I bought a new car yesterdayMy - cooking - mother - while - is - newspaper - a - dad - my - reading --> My mother is cooking while my dad is reading a newspaper

33. tolong buat kandialog bahasa inggris 2 orang dengan tema Instant noodle is good for your health.​


Jawaban:

Dialogue: Instant Noodle is Good for Your Health

Person A: Hey, have you heard the news that instant noodles are actually good for your health?

Person B: Really? I find that hard to believe. Instant noodles have always been known as an unhealthy food option.

Person A: I thought the same too, but apparently, there are some benefits to consuming instant noodles in moderation.

Person B: Okay, I'm intrigued. What are these supposed benefits?

Person A: Well, instant noodles provide a quick and convenient source of energy. The carbohydrates in the noodles give you an instant boost, especially during busy days or after a workout.

Person B: I guess that makes sense. It's good to have something quick to eat when you're in a rush. But what about the high sodium content?

Person A: Yes, instant noodles do tend to have high sodium levels, but you can manage that by drinking plenty of water and not adding the entire seasoning packet. You can also look for low-sodium versions or choose healthier toppings and ingredients.

Person B: That's a good point. I'll definitely keep that in mind. But what about the nutrition aspect?

Person A: Instant noodles can actually be enriched with various nutrients. Some brands have started adding vitamins and minerals to their products, making them more nutritious than before.

Person B: That's interesting. I've always assumed instant noodles lacked nutritional value.

Person A: They may not be a complete meal on their own, but if you add vegetables, lean proteins, or even eggs to your instant noodles, you can increase the overall nutritional value.

Person B: Ah, now I see. So, it's all about balance and making smart choices when it comes to instant noodles.

Person A: Exactly! Like any other food, moderation is key. If you enjoy instant noodles occasionally and pair them with nutritious ingredients, they can be a part of a balanced diet.

Person B: I appreciate you sharing this information. It's good to know that instant noodles can be enjoyed without feeling guilty.

Person A: No problem! It's important to have all the facts before making judgments about certain foods. Just remember to keep an eye on portion sizes and choose healthier options whenever possible.

Person B: Will do! Thanks again for enlightening me on this topic.

Person A: You're welcome! Enjoy your next bowl of instant noodles with a healthy twist!
