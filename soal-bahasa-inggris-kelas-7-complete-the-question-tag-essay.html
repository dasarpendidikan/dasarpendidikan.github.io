12. complete the sentences with the question tag. maya will visit me,....?​


~ Answer:won't she?

Maya will visit me, won't she?

.

~ Discussion:

Halo! Question tag berfungsi untuk seseorang yang memberikan suatu pernyataan kepada orang lain apakah pernyataan tersebut dipahami olehnya atau belum dan memastikan apakah pernyataan benar atau tidak. Pernyataannya dalam positif dan negatif akan tetapi akan diubah menjadi kalimat interogatif sebagai question (pertanyaan).

Untuk menyatakan question tag:

letakkan di akhir kalimat dengan memisahkannya pakai komaquestion tag digunakan berdasarkan subyeknyato be/to has/modal/aux digunakan seusai diberikannya subyek agar dapat membentuk pertanyaanpernyataan pada positif, maka pertanyaan jadi negatifpernyataan pada negatif, maka pertanyaan jadi positif

Perhatikan pernyataan Maya will visit me merupakan kalimat positif dengan subyek adalah Maya menggunakan will dan kata gantinya: she dengan kalimat positif —> negatif (will she —>

won't she?

23. Pengertian Question Tag dalam bahasa inggris dan juga contoh nya???


quetion tag is a question which is put at the end of the statement. example: Indah is smart, isn` t she? We use question tags in conversation and informal writing. A question tag consits of two words. We put them at the end of a statement to make a question. After a positive statement we use a negative tag. And vice versa. Ex: Do you eat chilies, don't you? He is a teacher, isn't he? She goes to school, doesn't she? He did his achievements, didn't he? Didn't he have a breakfast, did he?

Firstly, most of humanity’s collective knowledge is stored in libraries. Secondly, libraries protect and preserve this knowledge. They also classify or group the materials into logical and easily available divisions.
Furthermore, libraries make the materials available to everyone and even provide librarians to help us find what we need.
Finally, libraries are our link to the past and our gift to the future.
