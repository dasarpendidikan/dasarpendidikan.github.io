21. Jawab dengam penyelsaian


MEKANIKA

m2 = 7 kg
m1 = 2 kg
mk = 1 kg
k = 1 (silinder berongga)
g = 10 m/s²
T1?

penyelesaian:
Percepatan sistem
a = m1 g / (k mk + m1 + m2)
a = 2 . 10 / (1 . 1 + 2 + 7)
a = 20 / 10
a = 2 m/s²

Tegangan tali T1
m1 g - T1 = m1 a
20 - T1 = 2 . 2
20 - T1 = 4
T1 = 20 - 4
