1. Contoh karangan bahasa inggris our busy roads


Our Busy Road
In urban areas, a busy street is frequented by humans, land vehicles, and animals all day long. Busy streets are usually observed in the center of the community where it shelters the most members of the population. The reason behind is that streets are usually the center of trade for most of the owners of an enterprise or institution. A street becomes busy during rush hours such as in the morning between 6 am and 8 pm and then in the afternoon or evening between 5 pm to 8 pm. The streets are then filled with students, workers, business owners, and logistics operators who are either coming or go to school while passing the street. Busy streets never vacate any passing individuals or groups because this is the main passageway to accomplish tasks on the day. This means that the community has an active business structure and public services to the local residents and visitors in the area.

12. Tolong Dijawab!Mandiri Bahasa Inggris kelas 8,Practice 7 Halaman 100!Besok Dikumpul...


1. I went to the beach, but I didn't go to the lake.

2. We watched a horror film, but we didn't watch an action film.

3. Amora visited the museum, but we didn't visit the zoo.

4. Andre spoke to Yudi, but Andri didn't speak to Anita.

5. Lucy ate burgers, but Rudi didn't drink milk.

Pembahasan

Soal di atas memintamu untuk membuat kalimat dari kata-kata dan gambar yang diberikan. Pada intinya dalam mengerjakan soal tersebut kamu harus perhatikan bahwa tenses yang digunakan dalam membuat kalimat adalah simple past tense yang menceritakan kejadian pada waktu yang sudah lewat. Ciri-cirinya kamu lihat ada penggunaan "did not/didn't" dan kata kerja (verb) harus kamu tulis dalam bentuk past. Dalam kalimat di atas perubahan kalimatnya adalah:

1. Kata go (pergi) ubah menjadi went.  

2. Kata watch (menonton) ubah menjadi watched.

3. Kata visit (berkunjung) ubah menjadi visited.

4. Kata speak (bicara) ubah menjadi spoke.

5. Kata eat (makan) ubah menjadi ate.

Kata kerja sesudah did not harus berubah menjadi verb 1 (verb asal tanpa perubahan).

29. Apa yang dimaksud dengan Our Busy Roads?Tolong di jawab.​


Jawaban:

Chapter VI Bahasa Inggris kelas VIII, Our busy roads mengcover materi mengenai kosa kata tentang benda-benda di sekitar jalan raya, suasana dan perilaku manusia di sekitarnya. Tujuan atau goal dari bab ini adalah untuk menyatakan eksistensi dari benda-benda dan orang (to state the existence of things and people) dalam konteks di sekitar jalan raya. Namun demikian materi dapat meluas tidak hanya di jalan raya. Materi spesifik yang akan dipelajari pada chapter/bab ini adalah “There is” dan “there are” kemudian Quantifier seperti: “Many, Much, A few dan A little”. Materi tambahan dapat berupa countable dan uncountable nouns serta artikel atau kata sandang seperti a atau an dan the.

37. jawaban buku mandiri bahasa Inggris kelas 8 halaman 94 Pactice 1​


Write was or were in the blank spaces in the following passage.

Pada pertanyaan berikut kita diminta untuk menuliskan was atau were pada bagian yang kosong.

Pembahasan

Last month we were so excited beause we went on a vacation. It was a beautiful Saturday afternoon and the sun was really bright. Mum, dad, Marina and I were at Soekarno Hatta airport. The flight was at three in the afternoon. The airport was crowded and there were so many people who were waiting for their flight too. After waiting for a few hours, we were on the plane and ready to go to Yogyakarta. We were there for 5 days. It was the best vacation ever.

Untuk mengerjakan soal tersebut, perhatikan penggunaan subyeknya.

Penggunaan Was adalah untuk subyek berjumlah satu (I, She, He, It).Penggunaan Were apabila jumlah subyeknya banyak (They, We, You).
