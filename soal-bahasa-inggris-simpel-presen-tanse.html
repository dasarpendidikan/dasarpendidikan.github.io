4. apakah yg dimksd dgn simpel presen dlm b.inggris​


EXPLANATION

Simple present tense is a verb tense used to describe the general truth, the habit, the repeated activity and the fixed plan in the future.

The use of adverb of frequency is the easiest way in understanding the simple present form, among others : always, usually, often, everyday, seldom, rarely, daily, never, frequently, occasionally , ever, sometimes, regularly.

Pattern :

1. Verbal Simple Present

Verbal Sentence, a sentence contains a verb as a predicate.

(+) Subject + Verb(s/es) + Object/C

(-)  Subject + do/does +not + Verb 1 + Object/C

(?) Do/does+ Subject + Verb 1 + Object/C?

Example :

(+) The earth revolves around the sun    { General truth }

(+)  He usually plays football after school   { Habit }

(+)  She works at the hospital as a pediatrician   { Repeated activity }

2. Nominal Simple Present

Nominal sentence,  a sentence contains the copulative verb followed by noun/adjective/adverb.

(+ ) Subject + is/am/are +noun/adjective/adverb

(- )  Subject + is/am/are + not + noun/adjective/adverb

( ? ) Is/am/are+ subject +noun/adjective/adverb?

Example  :

(+) He is a surgeon.

(+) Mr. Jokowi is a President of Indonesia

Note :

If we use the singular subject { He, she, it, name of person and singular things }, we must add suffix -s/es in every verb of affirmative sentences.

Meanwhile, we don't need suffix -s/es if the subject is categorized in plural { they, we, I, you and plural things }.

13. Tolong b.inggris,,,Jawablah dengan benarPertanyaan: Tuliskan 3 contoh simpel present tanse, past tanse, dan perfect tanse...Contoh Kalimatnya Pada foto tersebut.​


Jawaban:

1) Contoh Kalimat Simple Present Tense: ( + ) Mrs. Wiranti teaches English everyday.

( - ) Mrs. Wiranti doesn't teach English everyday.

( ? ) Does Mrs. Wiranti teach English everyday?

( + ) We study English everyday.

( - ) We don't study English everyday.

( ? ) Do we study English everyday?

( + ) Angelina studies English everyday.

( - ) Angelina doesn't study English everyday.

( ? ) Does Angelina study English everyday?

2) Contoh Kalimat Simple Past Tense:

( + ) I went to Bali with my friends last week.

( - ) I did not go to Bali with my friends last week.

( ? ) Did I go to Bali with my friends last week?

( + ) We played martial arts yesterday.

( - ) We didn't play martial arts yesterday.

( ? ) Did we play martial arts yesterday?

( + ) Last year we went to Surabaya.

( - ) Last year we didn't go to Surabaya.

( ? ) Did we go to Surabaya last year?

3) Contoh Kalimat Present Perfect Tense:

( + ) We have been to Europe in all our life.

( - ) We have never been to Europe in all our life.

( ? ) Have we been to Europe in all our life?

( + ) He has finished his work.

( - ) He hasn't finished his work.

( ? ) Why hasn't he finished his work?

( + ) I have booked a hotel room for us.

( - ) I haven't booked a hotel room for us.

( ? ) Have I booked a hotel room for us?

22. membuat kalimat simpel present tanse dan simpel past tanse dengan kata mencintai?​


Tense Simple Present:

"I love you"

Tense Simple Past:

"I loved you"

atau bisa juga "I used to love you"

Artinya:

Dua-duanya memiliki arti yang sama, tetapi maknanya sedikit berbeda, pada kalimat simple present, artinya "Saya mencintaimu" (hingga saat dia bilang itu, dia masih mencintai orang yang dituju). Sedangkan pada kalimat simple past, artinya "Saya dulunya mencintaimu" (dia sudah tidak mencintai orang yang dituju, tetapi dulunya dia mencintai orang tersebut)

◈ Verbal Sentence

Verbal sentence (kalimat verba) adalah kalimat di mana predikatnya merupakan verb (kata kerja).

Pola Kalimat Verba:

(+) S + be (-) S + be + not (?) Be + S + ...?

Simple Present, Present Continuous Tense and Past Tense

Simple Present Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan habit (kebiasaan), unchanging situation, general truths and fixed arrangement.

Simple present dibentuk dengan menggunakan verb-1, tetapi jika subyeknya adalah orang ketiga tunggal malam pada verb-1 ditambahkan -s contohnya : take-takes, live-lives, eat-eats

contoh kalimat simple present:

He drinks coffee every morning.I work in Jakarta.Water freezes at zero degree.He catches the bus to school every morning.Mother cooks dinner every night.

Present Continuous Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang sedang dilakukan pada saat ini.

Verb yang digunakan adalah verb-1+ing dan auxilliary verb yang digunakan adalah is/am/are/

contoh kalimat present continuous tense:

He is writing a letter.I am washing the dishes.The students are studying in the classroom.People are walking in the street during the parade.I am enjoying the view in front of me.

Simpe Past Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan suatu kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau dan sudah selesai.

Bentuk umum dari Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2

(-) Subject + did not + infinitive without to

(?) Did + Subject + infinitive without to

Contoh kalimat simple past tense:

I went to the library yesterday.I didn't go to the library yesterday.Did I go to the library yesterday?Anna bought this car two weeks ago.Endra posted the letter three days ago.

Keterangan waktu yang menunjukkan simple past tense diantaranya yesterday,  a week ago, two days ago, this morning.

Simple Present Tense  

Simple present tense digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta /kebenaran umum (general truth), kebiasaan (habitual action), atau kejadian yang terjadi pada saat ini (present). Simple present tense paling sering digunakan dalam bahasa Inggris karena digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan sehari-hari.  

Formula:  

Verbal sentences:  

(+) Subject + Verb1 s/es  

(-) Subject + do/does + not + Verb1  

(?) Do/does + Subject + Verb1?  

Nominal sentence:  

(+) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Object/Complement  

(-) Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Object/Complement  

(?) To be (is/am/are) +Subject + Object/Complement?  

Contoh:  

1. (+) Susi brings her dictionary  

  (-) Susi does not bring her dictionary  

  (?) Does Susi bring her dictionary?

2. (+) My father drinks a cup of coffee every morning

   (-) My father does not drink a cup of coffee every morning

   (?) Does My father drink a cup of coffee every morning?

3. (+) Amy often swims in the swimming pool

   (-) Amy does not often swim in the swimming pool

   (?) Does Amy often swim in the swimming pool?

Present Continuous Tense

Penggunaan:

Present continuous tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang sedang berlangsung sekarang atau rencana dimasa depan.

Present continuous tense dibentuk dengan auxiliary verb “be”, berupa is/am/are – karena present tense (waktu sekarang),  dan present participle (-ing).  Is digunakan pada subject berupa singular noun (seperti: Tita, book) dan third person singular pronoun (seperti: she, he, it); are pada plural noun (seperti: boys, men, books), plural pronoun (seperti: we, they) dan you; sementara am pada I.

Rumus:

(+) S + am/is/are + present participle (-ing)

(-) S + am/is/are + not + present participle (-ing)

(?) am/is/are + S + present participle (-ing)?

Contoh Kalimat:

1. (+) I’m driving a car to Bandung now.

   (-) I’m not driving a car to Bandung now

   (?) Are you driving a car to Bandung now?

2. (+) The buses are arriving in an hour.

    (-) The buses are not arriving in an hour

    (?) Are the buses arriving in an hour?

3. (+) The teacher is explaining the lesson

   (-) The teacher is not explaining the lesson

   (?) Is the teacher explaining the lesson
