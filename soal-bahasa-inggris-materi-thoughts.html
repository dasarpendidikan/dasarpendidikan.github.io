Bahasa Inggris:

In English, opinion and thoughts are rather different in their definition. Opinion is basically a statement about what you have in mind about something, either agree or disagree, pro or con and so on. Thoughts are actually ideas or what you think about something. It can be an opinion, solution, critics etc.

Bahasa Indonesia:

Dalam bahasa Inggris, opini dan pemikiran memiliki definisi yang berbeda. Opini pada dasarnya adalah pernyataan tentang apa yang ada di benak Anda tentang sesuatu, baik setuju atau tidak setuju, pro atau kontra dan sebagainya. Pikiran sebenarnya adalah ide atau apa yang Anda pikirkan tentang sesuatu. Bisa berupa opini, solusi, kritik, dll.

33. opinions thoughts jelaskan tentang ini Dan bagai mana kah uraiyan nya


thoughts itu statis, sedangkan opinions itu aktif

thought itu adalah ide tentang objek tertentu dan ada dalam pikiran.

opinion mengungkapkan keyakinan tentang sesuatu yang dipegang dengan keyakinan dalam pikiran tetapi tidak dibuktikan dengan pengetahuan atau bukti positif dan cenderung diarahkan menuju akhir yang terencana di dalam pikiran.

(A thought is static, while an opinion is active.
A thought is an idea or a set of ideas about a particular object. it resides in the mind.
An opinion expresses a belief about something that is held with confidence in the mind but is not substantiated by positive knowledge or proof. It tends to be directed toward an envisioned end in the mind.)

Ini contoh dialogue opinions and thoughts tentang wildlife conservation/konservasi satwa liar

Dzaki : Hello, Akmal ! Do you know what is wildlife conservation ?

Akmal : Yes, I know, wildlife conservation is very important

Dzaki : Can you explain to me what is wildlife conservation ?

Akmal : Wildlife conservation is the practice of protecting plant and animal species and their habitats

Dzaki : Oh, I see.. that means we have to conserve their species from extinction

Akmal : Yes, that’s right

Dzaki : Why is wildlife conservation very important nowadays ?

Akmal : Wildlife conservation is very important nowadays because today, species of wild and exotic animals are in danger for example cendrawasih from Irian Jaya and Orangutan from Sumatra

Dzaki : And what is the reason behind this ?

Akmal : Humans are the reason behind this problem. They always continuously hunt them because of their greediness

Dzaki : So the government and other people should take some steps to prevent this extinction

Akmal : Yes, Dzaki you’re right. Goverment keep trying their best

Dzaki : Ok, thank you for your information Akmal

Akmal : You’re welcome Dzaki.
