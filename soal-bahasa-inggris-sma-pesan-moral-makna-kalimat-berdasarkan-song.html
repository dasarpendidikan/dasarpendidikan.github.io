31. tolong analisiskan 2 lagu dalam bahasa inggris, yang meliputi purpose, moral value, and to whom the song send?? tolong jawab menggunakan bahasa inggris y... makasih


Answer: 

OK, the songs chosen for this task are 'Yesterday' by The Beatles (also used for the OST of Mr.Bean movie, the first one) and 'For Rest of My Life' by Maher Zein.

'Yesterday' by The Beatles
-The purpose of the song is to state the singer's longing for the past when he was still with his beloved one happily. Now, he can only regret it as he misses it. 
-The moral value is that regrets always come at the end. So, don't make mistakes before you realise that you can't relive the past time. 
- The song is clearly for the singer's beloved one in the past and as the expression of regrets.


'For the Rest of my Life' by Maher Zein
- The purpose of the song is to show the singer's gratefulness to the God Almighty for having found his soulmate. 
- The moral value is that always grateful for the presence of our significant other, wife or husband as God has chosen her/him to be our eternal company. 
- The song is obviously for the singer's wife.
 
Kelas: SMA
Mapel: English
Kategori: song interpretation
