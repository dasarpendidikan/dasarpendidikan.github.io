For many years people believed that the cleverest animals after man were the chimpanzees. Now, however, there is a proof that dolphins may be even cleverer than these big apes.

Although a dolphin lives in the sea, it is not a fish. It is a mammal. It is in many ways, therefore, like a human being.

Dolphins have a simple language. They are able to talk to one another. It may be possible for man to learn how to talk to dolphins. But, this will not be easy because dolphins cannot hear the kind of sounds man can make. If man wants to talk to dolphins, therefore, he will have to make a third language which both he and the dolphins can understand.

Dolphins are also very friendly toward man. They often follow ships. There are many stories about dolphins guiding ships through difficult and dangerous waters.

Rose Flower

Rose is one of the most popular flowers in the world. It is native to Asia but has been widely cultivated and hybridized around the world. The scientific name for rose is Rosa, and there are over 100 species and thousands of cultivars available in different colors, sizes, and fragrances.

Roses are known for their beauty and sweet fragrance. They have been used as a symbol of love, beauty, and passion for centuries. Roses can be grown in different climates, but they require well-drained soil, regular watering, and pruning to maintain their shape and health.

Rose flowers are large and showy, with five petals and many stamens in the center. The petals can be single or double, and they come in various colors such as red, pink, yellow, white, and orange. The fragrance of the rose depends on the cultivar and can be described as spicy, fruity, or musky.

Rose flowers have many uses besides being ornamental. They are used in perfumes, cosmetics, and food flavorings. Rose petals can be dried and used in potpourri or brewed into tea for medicinal purposes. Roses are also used in wedding bouquets, corsages, and centerpieces.

Rose cultivation requires knowledge and care. Gardeners must choose the right location, soil, and type of rose for their area. They must also provide adequate water, fertilizer, and pest control. Pruning is important for shaping the plant and promoting new growth.

In conclusion, the rose is a beautiful and versatile flower that has captured the hearts of people around the world. Its beauty and fragrance are unparalleled, and its uses are numerous. Growing and caring for roses requires patience and knowledge, but the rewards are well worth the effort.

           Jellyfish are not really fish. They are invertebrate animals. This means that unlike fish or people, they have no backbones. In fact, they have no bones at all.

           Jellyfish have stomachs and mouths, but no heads. They have nervous systems for sensing the world around them, but no brains. They are made almost entirely of water, which is why you can look through them.

           Some jellyfish can glow in darkness by making their own light. The light is made by a chemical reaction inside the jellyfish. Scientists believe jellyfish glow for several reasons. For example, they may glow to scare away predators or to attract animals they like to eat.

           Most jellyfish live in salt water, apart from a few types that live in fresh water. Jellyfish are found in oceans and seas all over the world. They live in warm, tropical seas and in icy waters near the North and South poles.
