3. Tolong buatkan dialog berbahasa inggris yang memuat give suggestion to do and not to do something, invite someone to do and not to do something, and say agree or disagree with rules, suggestions, and invitations


1. to give suggestions 
TO DO 
1. i suggest you to buy food now ,because you might can't buy it later (alasannya)
2. i suggest you to submit the homework now, because if you don't our teacher will punish you
3. i suggest you to do your homework , because you might forget and the teacher will punish you
4. i suggest you to take your house key, because you might can't get in
5. i suggest you wear sneakers, because if you wear high heels your feet might get swollen 

NOT TO DO
1 i suggest you don't jump from that cliff, because you will die and we still love you
2. i suggest you not to fight with the others, because you might get detention or worst 
3. i suggest you not to go to the cafeteria, because it's packed with people
4. i suggest you not to play outside, because it's raining
5. i suggest you not to run, because your ankle is swollen

2. to invite someone to do or not to do something 
1. lets go shopping (to do )
2. let's get drinks
3. let's eat
4. please don't play football anymore
5. please don't do a diet anymore you're already skinny

3. to say agree or disagree 
1. i agree with the rules of this school because it will make us more dicipline
2. i agree with your suggestion because if i play in the rain i will be ill
3. i disagree with your invitation to do a demonstration, cause i don't have anything to complain about

Instruction to make an orange juice : 1. Prepare a mixer, an orange, sugar and ice cube. 2. Peel the orange and cut it into small pieces, then put it inside the mixer. 3. Pour 3 spoons of sugar and put all the ice cubes to make it fresh. 4. Close the mixer and turn on the mixer. Wait until the orange is blended well. 5. Open the mixer and pour the orange juice in a glass. Your orange juice is ready to serve !

18. tolong jelaskan materi bahasa inggris yang asking someone to do something

Contoh: Can you turn off the fan?
