20. Listen and repeat dialog 1 terjemahan inggris ke indonesia


Listen and repeat dialogue 1

Dengarkan dan ulangi percakapan satu

____________________

Baik listen dan repeat merupakan kata kerja atau verb, sedangkan dialogue merupakan kata benda atau noun

Listen

V1 = Listen

V2 = Listened

V3 = Listened

Meaning = Mendengar/mendengarkan

Repeat

V1 = Repeat

V2 = Repeated

V3 = Repeated

Meaning = Mengulangi

Dialogue

Singular noun = Dialogue

Plural noun = Dialogues

Meaning = Percakapan

Jawaban:

Childrens

Penjelasan:

Kerana para anak, kata para berarti tidak tunggal pake alias jamak jdi "the childrens listen".

semoga membantu

Jawaban dan Penjelasan:

Karena "the children" pada kalimat tersebut adalah subjek jamak, dimana subjeknya lebih dari 1 (the children = they). Sementara, untuk penggunaan v1+s seperti listens, hanya dipakai untuk subjek tunggal, yaitu she, he, dan it.

26. bahasa Inggris soal 1. he is watering the flower(-)(+)2. listen I your father is talking(-)(?)she is practicing a concert(-)(?)Jone sure flying his life wow(+)(?)(bantu jawab tolong,, kayak contoh yg diatas​


Jawaban:1. (-) He is not watering the flower (?) is He watering the flower?

2. (-) i and your father is not talking (?) is i and your father talking?

3. (-) She is not practicing a concert (?) is she practicing a concert?

4. (-) Jone is not sure flying his life wow (?) Is Jone sure flying his life? wow

Penjelasan:

(+) = S + Is,am,are + O/C

(-) = S + is,am,are + not + O/C

(?) = is,am,are + S + O/C + ?

Is digunakan untuk she,he,it

am digunakan untuk i

are digunakan untuk you,we,they

29. apa bahasa indonesianya serios why listen


Jawaban:

1. Serious : Serius

Example : " Can't you 'be serious' on this task ?! "

"Bisakah kau 'serius' dalam tugas ini ?! "

2. Why : Kenapa / Mengapa

Example : " Why did your burn my books ?! "

" Kenapa kau membakar bukuku ?! "

3.Listen : Dengar / Mendengarkan / Dengarkan

Example : " Didn't your already 'listened' the songs that one of the Bts member sang ? "

" Bukankah kau sudah mendengar lagu yang dinyanyikan oleh salah satu member Bts ? "
