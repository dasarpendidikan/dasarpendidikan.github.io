8. how many kinds of announcemen mention it and explain


There are many kinds of announcements, but they're all public and meant to inform. In school, the announcements might say whose birthday it is and what clubs are meeting after school. In a store, an announcement might mention a sale or a lost kid who's looking for her parents. The President makes lots of formal announcements, like the decision to run for re-election. So basically, if it's announcing something, it's an announcement.

1.Identification :Dalam bagian ini , kita mengidentifikasi orang , tempat/ benda yang akan dideskripsikan ( introduce the subject)
2.Description :Pembahasan tentang topik yang ada di identification ( Describe the features of the subject)An announcement is a public statement that's usually formal and has a specific purpose.

1. Stating Purpose 
2. Stating Day and Date and time

announcement principal consists of:

1. the purpose of the announcement, which is about why the announcement was made

2. the contents of the announcement: i.e. the content that will be informed in the announcement

3. target announcement: to whom the announcement was made

4. language announcement: what language will be used; formaal, semi-formal, non-formal

5. media announcements: what type of media to use: print, electronic
