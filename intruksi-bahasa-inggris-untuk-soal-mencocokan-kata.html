1. Wet your hands with water.

2. Pour enough soap on your hands to cover all surfaces of the hands.

3. Rub one palm to the other palm.

4. Rub the back of the hand and between the fingers.

5. Rub the back of the fingers into the palm of the hand with the position of the fingers interlocked.

6grasp and flush your thumb in a circular position.

7. Rub the tips of your fingers into your palms so that the nails are exposed to soap.

How to Wash Hands 7 Steps to Use Soap that is Good and Right

1. Wet the palms of the hands as high as the middle of the arm using running water, take soap then wipe and gently rub the palms

2. Also rub and rub the backs of your hands alternately

3. Do not forget the fingers, rub between fingers clean

4. Clean the tips of your fingers alternately by clasping

5. Rub and turn your thumbs alternately

6. Place the tip of the finger into the palm of the hand then rub gently

7. Clean both wrists alternately by rotating, then end by rinsing all parts of the hand with clean running water then dry using a towel or tissue.

14. intruksi cara cuci tangan menggunakan bahasa inggris​


How to properly wash your hands.

First of all, you have to prepare three main things: soap, clean water, and a towel. Please remember that the water must be flowing water to safely wash all the dirt and disease away. Second, pour water into your hands completely, and wash your hands with soap. Don't forget to wash between your finger. Do this washing for at least 30 seconds. Next, wash your hands with water cleanly so there's no soap left on your hands. And finally, dry your hands with a towel.Pembahasan

Membuat teks yang berisi instruksi termasuk ke dalam procedural text, yaitu teks yang berisi urutan proses melaksanakan suatu kegiatan atau proses membuat sesuatu.

Ada ciri khas kata-kata yang bisa digunakan dalam membuat procedural text, yaitu kita membuat kata-kata penghubung seperti next. Kita dapat menggunakan next sebagai penghubung  untuk merujuk pada sesuatu yang mengikuti segera setelah sesuatu sebelumnya. Kata-kata di awal instruksi bisa berupa: first of all.

Kita bisa pula menggunakan kata-kata : First, second, third, dan seterusnya untuk menunjukkan urutan instruksi. Untuk instruksi terakhir bisa kita pakai: finally (akhirnya) untuk menutup instruksi.

Semua procedural text di atas kita terapkan pada teks instruksi yang kita buat di atas, seperti ini terjemahannya:

Cara mencuci tangan yang benar.

Pertama-tama, Anda harus menyiapkan tiga hal utama: sabun, air bersih, dan handuk. Harap diingat bahwa air harus mengalir untuk membersihkan semua kotoran dan penyakit dengan aman. Kedua, tuangkan air ke tangan Anda sepenuhnya, dan cuci tangan Anda dengan sabun. Jangan lupa untuk mencuci sela-sela jari Anda. Lakukan pencucian ini setidaknya selama 30 detik. Selanjutnya, cuci tangan dengan air bersih agar tidak ada sisa sabun di tangan. Dan terakhir, keringkan tangan Anda dengan handuk.

21. Cara pembuatan Coco drink beserta unsurnya dalam bahasa Inggris


Jawaban:

MAKING PROCESS

In the process of making nata de coco, the materials needed include :

Materials:

100 liters of coconut water

100 grams (gr) of sugar

500 grams (gr) ZA

50 milliliters (ml) of vinegar/ acetic acid

While the process of making nata de coco consists of 2 stages, namely:

Making nata de coco:

filtering 100 liters of coconut water with a filter cloth. This filter aims to free coconut water from unwanted impurities.

adding 100 grams of sugar and 500 grams of ZA, then heating to a boil. Heating is done to boiling in order to kill microorganisms that might contaminate the product to be produced.

then, cooled down to room temperature. Once cool, the coconut water is placed in a sterile container

Add 50 milliliters of vinegar. The addition of vinegar serves to adjust the acidity level until a pH of 4-5 is obtained.

The addition of starter bacteria and incubated (scorched) for 2 weeks. In this ripening, the container is tightly closed with plastic. The best curing temperature is 30 0C.

Coconut water that has coagulated produces nata de coco which is ready to be harvested.

Penjelasan:

The nutritional content of Nata De Coco, according to research conducted by the LIPI Research and Development Center for Biology, per 100 grams of nata has a nutritional content of 80% water, 20 grams of carbohydrates, 146 cal calories, 12 mg of calcium, 20 grams of fat, 2 mg of phosphorus and also 0.5 mg Ferrum (iron).
