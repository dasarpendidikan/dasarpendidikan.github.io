4. Contoh kalimat tersirat dan tersurat dalam bahasa Inggris


→ Contoh kalimat tersurat:

This bottle is made of crystal glass. Botol ini terbuat dari kaca kristal.

→ Contoh kalimat tersirat:

The light that falls on the surface of this bottle becomes sparkling because the surface of the bottle, which is not ordinary glass, is so clear and sparkling. Cahaya yang jatuh ke permukaan botol ini menjadi berkilau karena permukaanya yang bukan kaca biasa begitu bening dan gemerlap. Pembahasan

Kalimat tersurat adalah kalimat yang maknanya langsung diketahui dari kalimatnya tanpa harus dimengerti dahulu. Ada pun kalimat tersirat adalah kalimat yang maknanya baru bisa diketahui setelah membaca keseluruhan kalimat secara lengkap atau setelah kita memahami pengertian yang tersusun dalam kalimat tersebut. Seperti contoh di atas pada kalimat pertama kita ketahui makna kalimat langsung yaitu:

This bottle is made of crystal glass. Botol ini terbuat dari kaca kristal.

Kita tidak perlu menafsirkan apa-apa karena kalimat tersebut langsung memberitahu kita bahwa botol tersebut terbuat dari kaca kristal.

Pada kalimat kedua yang berupa kalimat tersirat, maknanya baru akan kita ketahui setelah kita membaca dan memahami keseluruhan kalimat tersebut.

The light that falls on the surface of this bottle becomes sparkling because the surface of the bottle, which is not ordinary glass, is so clear and sparkling. Cahaya yang jatuh ke permukaan botol ini menjadi berkilau karena permukaanya yang bukan kaca biasa begitu bening dan gemerlap.

Dalam kalimat itu kita harus memahami dulu kenapa botol tersebut berkilau terkena cahaya, dan sangat gemerlap sebelum sampai pada pemahaman bahwa botol tersebut terbuat dari kaca kristal.

6. Contoh soal surat pribadi dalam bahasa inggris


Private Letter

Surat pribadi adalah surat yang ditulis secara tidak resmi yang mempounyai banyak tujuan, antara lain:

Memberi kabar dan menanyakan kabar

Menceritakan pengalaman

Generic Structure dari Private Letter adalah:

1. Date: It is the month, day and year that the letter is written on.

2. Greeting: Some times we call it “salutation”. It is the introductory phrase. It can be a phrase like:

Dear Tono, Dear My friend, Dear My Brother, etc

Body: All kinds of letter formal or informal have the letter body. This is the complete text of the letter or the subject matter content.

3. Closing: It is the farewell phrase. That is a word that precedes the signature and is followed by a comma. The phrases can be like; Yours truly, Sincerely, Respectfully yours, Regards, etc.

4. Signature: This the place where you writ your name and signature.

Language features dari Private letter adalah:

Use simple present tense, untuk menanyakan kabar maupun memberi kabar.

Use simple past tense, saat digunakan untuk menceritakan pengalaman

Use informal language, menggunakan bahasa tidak formal.

Pembahasan

Membuat private letter.

Lampung, December 12th, 2018

Dear Ratih,

Hello my friend, how are you doing, there? I hope you are in good condition. I am sending this letter to give you some good news.  

It's been one year we separated, since you moved to Makassar. I miss you so much. I'm really looking forward to meeting with you soon. After waiting for so long, i finally granted permission to visit you in Makassar. Maybe next month i will go there by plane. When i got there, you have to take me around to the tourist attractions there. I really wanted to visit the Losari beach. See you there buddy.

Your friend,

Penny

7. apa yang dimaksud dengan makna tersurat dan tersirat. Berserta contohnya?


Makna tersurat adalah makna yg terlihat jelas ada pada kalimat

Makna tersirat adalah makna yg dapat diambil dari makna suatu kalimat
Tersurat : disajikan pertanyaan, dan jawaban dari pertanyaan tersebut ada di teks yang tersedia.

Tersirat : disajikan pertanyaan, tetapi jawaban tersebut tidak ada di dalam teks. Kita yang menyimpulkan sendiri.

contoh :
Pada hari Senin, 30 April Adi bangun tidur karena ada suara ayam berkokok.

Tersurat : Tanggal berapa Adi bangun ?
Tanggal 30 April

Tersirat : kapan Adi bangun ?
Pada pagi hari (karena ada suara ayam berkokok)

36. contoh surat tentang liburan dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya (surat nya yang agak panjang)


Dear Regina,

How are you? I hope you’re fine. I’m writing this letter to tell you that I really miss you. Do you miss me too? It’s too bad that we couldn’t spend the last holiday together. I spent my holiday in Malang. I visited some places in the city, but among those places, Mount Bromo left the greatest impression on me.

Let me tell you how my tour in Mount Bromo went. I spent about 3 hours to get to Mount Bromo from Malang by public transportations. I arrived at the location in the afternoon. Since I wanted to watch the sunrise from the top of Penanjakan Hill which was one of the best sunrise spot in Mount Bromo, I decided to take a rest at a guest house and would go to the hill at the dawn. I woke up at 3 in the morning and as soon as I was ready, I rent a jeep to get to Penanjakan Hill. When arriving at the hill, many people were there. Long story short, I was finally at the top of the hill and witnessed the remarkable view of the golden sunrise. I spent around 1 hour there just enjoying the breathtaking view which the nature offered.

After that, I went to Savannah Hill also known as the Telletubbies Hill of Bromo by the same jeep. Again, the view was just so green and bizarre. It felt peaceful in there. Then, the jeep took me to the Whispering Sands. It may have been only the sea of sands, but the atmosphere there was so surreal. I felt like I was visiting the different planet. The next destination was the Bromo Crater itself. The jeep dropped me off at the jeep parking area around 1 to 2 km from the crater. I could either walk or ride a horse to get to the crater. I chose walking instead, because the crater was on the top of the mountain and I felt bad seeing the horses taking people on their back while climbing. It was actually an easy climbing. It only took less than 30 minutes to the top. On the top of the mountain, it was not the crater that stole my attention, but the scenery. Everyone and everything looked so tiny from there. I believe that if you’d been there, you’d have said something philosophical like, “human was only a speck of dust in the universe”

It was great experience, but it’d have been greater and more fun if you’d been there. That’s all I can tell you about my holiday. I’ll tell you the rest another time. Now that I’ve shared mine, why don’t you share yours?

I hope you write me back soon.

Love,

Karen
