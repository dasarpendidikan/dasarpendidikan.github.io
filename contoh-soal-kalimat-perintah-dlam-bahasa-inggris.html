6. Apa yg dimaksud dgn kalimat perintah,saran,ajakan,dan larangan dlam kebahasaan puisi rakyat ?


Jawaban:

Kalimat perintah adalah kalimat yang berisi atau bermaksud memberi perintah atau keseluruhan.

Contoh:

Buanglah sampah pada tempatnya!.

Kalimat saran adalah kalimat yang berisi tentan saran kepada orang lain untuk kebaikan orang lain ( sebaiknya, seharusnya ).

Contoh:

- Sebaiknya kau pikirkan dahulu

- Seharusnya kau memberi garam pada sup itu.

Kalimat ajakan adalah kalimat yang berisi ajakan kepada orang lain untuk suatu perbuatan ( ayo dan mari ).

Contoh:

Marilah kita jaga agar lestari

Kalimat larangan adalah kalimat yang berisi larangan agar orang laib tidak melakukan kegiatan ( jangan,hindari ).

Contoh:

Janganlah ber prasangka buruk kepada sesama.

Semoga membantu

13. Tolong buat kalimat aktif dan di ubah ke kalimat pasif dlam bahasa inggris serta terjemahannya,,,, 5 aja


Passive Voice

Buatlah kalimat aktif lalu diubah menjad kalimat pasif Bahasa Inggris sebanyak 5 kalimat!

Jawaban

1.

My mom waters the flowers. [K. aktif]

The flowers are watered by my mom. [K. pasif]

Artinya ↓

Ibuku menyirami bunga-bunga. [K. aktif]

Bunga-bunga disirami oleh Ibuku. [K. pasif]

--------------

2.

She makes breakfast every morning. [K. aktif]

Breakfast is made by her every morning. [K. pasif]

Artinya ↓

Dia membuat sarapan setiap pagi. [K. aktif]

Sarapan dibuat olehnya setiap pagi. [K. pasif]

--------------

3.

You always close the door. [K. aktif]

The door is always closed by you. [K. pasif]

Artinya ↓

Kamu selalu menutup pintu. [K. aktif]

Pintu selalu ditutup olehmu. [K. pasif]

--------------

4.

We play chess every day. [K. aktif]

Chess is played by us. [K. pasif]

Artinya ↓

Kami bermain catur setiap hari. [K. aktif]

Catur dimainkan oleh kami setiap hari. [K. pasif]

--------------

5.

They don't give the money. [K. aktif]

The money isn't given by them. [K. pasif]

Artinya ↓

Mereka tidak memberikan uang. [K. aktif]

Uang tidak diberikan oleh mereka. [K. pasif]

___________

Penjelasan

Kalimat di jawaban dibuat menggunakan rumus kalimat pasif 'simple present tense'. Rumusnya adalah sebagai berikut.

(+) S + is/am/are + V3 + by + A

(-) S + isn't/am not/aren't + V3 + by + A

*S = Subject; V = Verb; A = Agent.

A SMALL THING COSTS MUCH

One day, while walking down the road, a father said to his son, “Look, a horsesh is lying in the mud. Pick it up.”

The son said, “I hate to pick up things from mud.”

The father picked up the horsesh and kept it in his pocket. On reaching the village, he sold the horsesh and bought some cherries.

Father understood the desire of his son and dropped a cherry on the ground. The son picked it up, washed it and ate it up.  

Then the father said to him, “Sometimes even small things cost much. These cherries are because of that horsesh that you refused to pick up from the mud.”

The son understood what his father meant.

25. contoh teks eksposisi tentang nasi goreng dlam bahasa inggris​


Dalam Bahasa Inggris

Fried Rice is Indonesia traditional food. It is very popular food. It is easy to make it. It doesn't take too long time to make. The ingredients are easy to get.

How to make Fried Rice? The following is the procedure text how to make fried rice.

Ingredients:

2 cloves of Garlic

2 onions cut into small pieces

1 table spoon of vegetable oil

a plate of rice

some salt

Steps how to make it

First, pound garlic and 2 pinches of salt.

Second, heat vegetable oil on a frying pan.

Third, put the pounded garlic and salt, and onions into the hot vegetable oil. Fry them a while.

Fourth, put one plate of rice. Fry and mix them around 3 menit.

Fifth, add two pinches of salt. Continue frying until the rice is hot enough.

Finally, put the fried rice on a plate. Serve with a sunny-side up.

Descriptive text is a text which presents information about something specifically. The purpose of descriptive text is to describe a particular person or thing or place specifically.

Pembahasan:

Teks deskripsi tentang penyebab dan penanggulangan virus corona yaitu

COVID-19

COVID-19 is a new illness that can affect your lungs and airways. It’s caused by a type of coronavirus. COVID-19, like the flu, can be spread from person to person.

When a person who has COVID-19 coughs, sneezes or talks, they may spread droplets containing the virus a short distance, which quickly settle on surrounding surfaces.

What can you do to stop COVID-19 spreading? First, cough or sneeze into your elbow or by covering your mouth and nose with tissues. Put used tissues in the bin or a bag immediately. Wash your hands with soap and water often (for at least 20 seconds).

Try to avoid close contact with people who are unwell. Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth if your hands are not clean. Avoid personal contact, such as kissing, sharing cups or food with sick people. Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces and objects, such as doorknobs. And the last, stay home if you feel unwell.
