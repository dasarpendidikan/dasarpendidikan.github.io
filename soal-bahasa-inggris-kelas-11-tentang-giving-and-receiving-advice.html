18. tolong buatin dialog percakapan asking and giving advice tentang photography animasi dll(pilih salah satu) dialog NY dalam bahasa inggris​

Penjelasan:

Here is an example of a conversation asking and giving advice about photography:

A: Hi, I'm interested in photography and I want to improve my skills. Do you have any advice for me?

B: Sure, I'd be happy to help. What kind of photography are you interested in?

A: I'm interested in landscape and nature photography.

B: Great! One thing you can do is to study the work of other photographers in that genre. Look at their composition, lighting, and use of color. You can also practice by going out and taking photos yourself. Try experimenting with different angles and perspectives to create unique shots.

A: That's a good idea. Do you have any recommendations for camera settings?

B: It depends on the lighting and the effect you want to achieve. For landscape photography, you may want to use a smaller aperture to create a larger depth of field and capture more detail. You can also try using a slower shutter speed to create a sense of motion in waterfalls or clouds.

A: Thanks for the tips. Do you have any recommendations for editing software?

B: There are many options out there, but some popular ones include Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop, as well as free options like GIMP and RawTherapee. You can experiment with different software to find the one that works best for you.

A: That's helpful. Thanks for your advice!

B: You're welcome. Remember to keep practicing and experimenting, and don't be afraid to try new things. Good luck with your photography!

27. Asking, giving, and refusing suggestion and advice


A. Asking suggestion

Contoh

1. Do you have any suggestion for me?

2. Please tell me what should I do?

3. What would you advise me to do?

B. Giving Suggestion

1. How about doing some more exercise?

2. I would suggest doing more exercise.

3. I would recommend doing more exercise.

C. GIVING ADVISE

1. You had better do more exercise before you start getting fat.

2. You had better not drink so much beer or you will get fat

C. refusing suggetion/advise

I don’t think so.

I tried that, but it didn’t work.

Thanks, but that won’t help because….

I don’t want to do that because….

That’s a good idea, but….

I’m afraid I can’t do that.

I think it can’t solve my problem.
