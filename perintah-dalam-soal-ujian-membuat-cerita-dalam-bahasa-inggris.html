Cerita rakyat yaitu merupakan cerita yang berasal dari masyarakat jaman dahulu, yang sering diperkenalkan kepada generasi berikutnya yaitu secara turun temurun.

Jadi, cerita rakyat tersebut sudah diwariskan atau disebarluaskan yaitu secara lisan, yaitu melalui mulut ke mulut dan juga secara turun menurun.

Uncle: Good morning A, I am harvesting a lot of crops right now.

Uncle: There are corn, turnip, strawberry, bellpepper and many more, you can check it, do you want to help me harvest them?

Uncle: Experience makes it perfect! You can learn it too if you want.

Uncle: Of course, we can eat it all by ourself.

A: How much money you can make for selling it all?

Uncle: I think it's around $5000-$10000.

A: That's a lot of money! Maybe I should start learning it from you so I can make a lot of money.
