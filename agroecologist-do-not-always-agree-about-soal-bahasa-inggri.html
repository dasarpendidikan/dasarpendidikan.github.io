Orang setuju dengan saya adalah karena mereka perhatian, pengecut, atau kecerdikan.

Pembahasan:

Mengapa beberapa orang setuju dengan anda, mengetahui bahwa mereka tidak bersungguh-sungguh?

Perhatian, pengecut, atau kecerdikan.

Orang yang bijaksana mungkin setuju dengan saya karena mereka menyadari bahwa, meskipun saya bodoh dan tidak dapat menemukan pantat saya dengan kedua tangan dan unit GPS, mereka tidak ingin berdebat jika mereka menyinggung ego saya yang rapuh.

Orang-orang pengecut mungkin memiliki ide-ide yang mereka akan sulit untuk membuktikan dan setuju dengan saya daripada mengambil risiko memiliki lubang di sesuatu yang mereka pegang dekat dengan hati mereka.

Orang-orang yang cerdas menyadari bahwa saya adalah seorang pengecut tua yang akan berpegang teguh pada ide-ide kuno di hadapan semua bukti yang bertentangan. Mereka tidak repot-repot membuang napas.

8. Dialog bahasa inggris tentang most people do not agree


*( Dialog 1 )*

A: I was thinking of spending our holiday in the mountains.

B: I think so too!

A: I was thinking it could take place sometime in September.

B: I'm not sure about that! That might be a little too cold for some people to go there in September.

A: Really? Hmm I don’t have any enough information about mountain.

B: What about in the end of April? April has good weather, doesn’t it?

A: Yes, absolutely, that would work out better.

B: You know, maybe it’s better if we start finding some information about Mount Bromo or others.

A: I couldn't agree more! We'll have to get right on it.

B: Fine! Well, let’s do it now!

*( Dialog 2 )*

A: I think that it will be great if we spend our New Year eve in the beach this year.

B: What a great idea! I couldn’t agree more.

C: I don’t think so, there will be so many people there.

A: Ya I know, but December would be a good month for spending time together.

B: What about making a private celebration in y home? We can do many activities together freely.

C: That is a good suggestion. There is a wide garden in your house, isn’t there?

A: I'd go along with that, but we must prepare it together well from now. I don’t need to burden B.

B: Ok I’m on your side.

C: Yes, I do think so.

*( Dialog 3 )*

A: hi guys !

B: hi A !

C: hi A !

D: hi A !

A: hey, B, yesterday you said that we would have to discuss a great plan the following day. What’s that?

D: I’m so curious to know about it. Please tell us.

C: yeah, tell us about it now

B: Ok, it’s actually about our class. I need to make our class more colorful. Do you agree?

A: Yes i do. I agree with you.

C: I am not sure, I am afraid that our class advisor will be angry with us if we don’t ask her first.

B: how about you, D?

D: I agree up to a point, but I think what was said by C is right. We have to discuss it not only with our class advisor but also with our classmates.

B: Yeah that’s right, i think i it’s good idea but I’m not confident enough to present this idea in the class.

C: Really? Hmm let me do it and everyone must agree with me hehehe

A: No,  no, no! No need to be arrogant please. I disagree with you. We must also hear our classmate’s opinion.  

B: Yeah, that’s right.

C: Ok no problem.

D: What a great plan, let’s make it real as soon as possible!

B: Ok!

C: OK!

A: Ok!

31. You . . . always . . . rice for breakfast.do not - atedoes not - atedo not - eatdoes not - eat​


Jawaban:

DO NOT - EAT

Penjelasan:

DO NOT :

"DO" untuk subjek I/YOU/THEY/WE yang di mana subjek di pertanyaan di atas adalah YOU

DOES NOT :

"DOES" untuk subjek HE/SHE/IT tetapi subjek di pertanyaan di atas adalah YOU

EAT :

"EAT" adalah verb 1 dimana verb yang di gunakan di pertanyaan diatas adalah verb 1

ATE :

"ATE" adalah verb 2 tetapi veb yang di gunakan di pertanyaan di atas adalah verb 1
