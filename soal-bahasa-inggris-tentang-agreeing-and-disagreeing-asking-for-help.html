8. Tolong buatin contohnya plis saling berhubungan gitu. asking for opinion---giving opinion---agreeing---disagreeing---​


Asking for opinion:

1. Person A: Hey, have you tried the new restaurant in town? What do you think of it?

2. Person B: How was the movie you saw last night? Would you recommend it?

3. Person C: I'm thinking of buying a new smartphone. Any recommendations?

Giving opinion:

1. Person A: Personally, I really enjoyed the food at the new restaurant. The flavors were unique and the service was excellent.

2. Person B: I thought the movie was fantastic! The plot was engaging, and the acting was top-notch.

3. Person C: In my opinion, the XYZ smartphone is a great choice. It has a powerful processor and an impressive camera.

Agreeing:

1. Person A: I agree with you, the new restaurant is definitely worth a visit. The ambiance and the quality of the food are outstanding.

2. Person B: I couldn't agree more! The movie was a masterpiece. It had me on the edge of my seat throughout.

3. Person C: I'm on the same page as you. The XYZ smartphone offers excellent features and value for money.

Disagreeing:

1. Person A: I have a different opinion about the new restaurant. I found the food to be overpriced and the service to be slow.

2. Person B: I respectfully disagree. I didn't find the movie to be as impressive as people say. It lacked depth in its storytelling.

3. Person C: Personally, I wouldn't recommend the XYZ smartphone. I think there are better options available with more advanced features.

Jawaban:

Asking for opinion:

1. What do you think about the new restaurant in town?

2. How do you feel about the latest fashion trend?

3. What is your opinion on the current political situation?

Giving opinion:

1. I personally think the new restaurant in town has great food and a cozy ambiance.

2. In my opinion, the latest fashion trend is unique and stylish.

3. I believe that the current political situation requires immediate attention and reform.

Agreeing:

1. I completely agree with you, the new restaurant is definitely worth trying.

2. I couldn't agree more, the latest fashion trend is refreshing and suits different body types.

3. I share the same opinion as you, the current political situation is concerning and needs to be addressed.

Disagreeing:

1. I respectfully disagree, I found the new restaurant's food to be mediocre.

2. I have a different opinion, I don't think the latest fashion trend is suitable for everyone.

3. I understand your perspective, but I disagree with your opinion on the current political situation.

Exactly!

Disagreeing

Partly agreeing
