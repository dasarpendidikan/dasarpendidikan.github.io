Asking & giving permissionWrite sentences about ask permission and the respond.

1. May I borrow your pillow?

➡ Sure.

2. Can I borrow your bolster?

➡ For sure, take it easy.

3. Would you mind if I use your study lamp?

➡ No, I wouldn't. Just use it.

4. Am I able to use your prayer rug / carpet?

➡ Yes, you're able.

5. Could I use your table?

➡ Ah, I'm sorry. I'm going to use that table too, sorry.

6. Can I turn on the fan?

➡ No, you're not permitted. I'm sorry, but I feel a bit shivering.

7. Is it possible for me to take your blankets/pillows/beds?

➡ Yes, of course.

8. May I use your slippers?

➡ Sorry, you can't. I'm about to use it too.

PembahasanI. Pengertian

Asking for permission adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk meminta izin saat kita ingin memakai/meminjam/melakukan sesuatu yang berhubungan dengan orang lain.

Giving a permission adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberi suatu izin (mengizinkan) atas permintaan yang kita terima sebelumnya.

II. Contoh

Contoh-contoh dari asking a permission, giving a permission terdapat di kalimat jawaban di atas.
