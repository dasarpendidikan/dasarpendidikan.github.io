20. apa perbedaan dan perbandingan struktur kalimat bahasa inggris simple present tense, simple past tense , simple future tense dan present perfect tense dengan struktur kalimat bahasa indonesia ?

Penjelasan:

1. simple present tense

pola nya:

verb: s+verb 1 + obj

ex: i go to school

non verb : s+ tobe (is, am, are) +adjective/adverb

ex: i am beautiful

2. simple past tense

pola nya:

verb: s+ verb 2+ obj

ex: i went to school

non verb: s+ tobe (was, were)+ adjective/adverb

ex: i was smart

3. simple future tense

polanya:

verb: s + Will/shall + verb 1 + obj

ex: i Will help you

non verb: s+Will/shall + be + adjective /adverb

ex: i Will be smart

4. present perfect tense

polanya:

verb: s +have/has+ verb 3+obj

ex: i have gone to the market

non verb: s+ have/ has + Been + adjective/adverb

ex: we have Been here


21. soal pilihan ganda tentang past tense vs present perfect


Simple Past Tense vs Present Perfect Tense

Simple Past Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung di waktu lampau.

Keterangan waktu yang sering digunakan dalam simple past tense antara lain:

last week/ night/ month/ yearyesterdayonce upon a timeone daythis morningdan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan dalam simple past tense adalah:

(+)S + V2

(-)S + did not + V1  

(?) Did + S + V1


Present Perfect Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian sebelum sekaran (lampau). Perbedaan Present Perfect Tense dengan Simple Past Tense adalah: Simple Past Tense memerlukan kata keterangan waktu yang jelas, sedangkan Present Perfect Tense tidak.

Namun, biasanya dalam present perfect tense menggunakan kata since dan for.

Since diikuti dengan waktu yang jelas, seperti since 1992, since monday, dan lain-lain.

For diikuti dengan durasi waktu, seperti for a week, for ten years, dan lain lain.

Haunted theatre
Last week, me and my family went to XXI in Kuningan city mall of Jakarta. When we arrived at the mall, we directly rushed to XXI cinema and bought the tickets. After we bought the tickets, we went to have lunch. We had our lunch in Ichiban sushi. We checked the time and it's our turn to watch so we decided to hurry and go to the cinema. When we arrived, the cinema was very deserted and quiet. Then we finally realized that there was no one. Not a single soul except the silent waiter, the ticket cashier and us. The atmosphere was really quiet and my mother was scared so we entered the the studio quickly. Inside the studio, there was no one again. I started to feel the chills and cringed. Not long later the lamps was turned off and the movie began. During the movie, I felt something or someone touched me. I was curious so I looked behind. I saw a shadow of a man without the person. I thought my minds was playing tricks with me so i just forget about the shadow and enjoyed the movie. After the movie finished, I asked my mother and father whether they had felt someone touched them. They said that they also felt the same. Suddenly, a nearby lamp explodes by itself! Me and my family was really shocked and ran away to the parking lot. We rushed home as fast as we can. simple present tense :
a tense that is used when an action is happening right now or when it happens regularly.
characteristic :
root form & ending in s or es

simpe present perfect tense :
a tense that is used to talk about a finished action or situation.
characteristic :
auxiliary verb (has & have) & verb 3
