17. Ayu always milk before she goes to school TOLONG DIJAWAB YANG BENAR YA INI SOAL BAHASA INGGRIS KELAS 5


To complete the sentence, we need to use simple present verb. The reason why we need simple present tense is because of the frequency adverbs 'always'. Simple present tense is used when we're talking about our daily routines or something that is done repeatedly. We can also use simple present to state a fact or something that is already true.

Discussion

The sentence that needs to be completed is Ayu always ____ milk before she goes to school.

The pattern for simple present is 'subject + verb + time marker'. The verb changes depending on the subject used on the sentence.

For example:

- Nisa eats toast every morning.

- I drink coffee every morning.

- They play basketball every Tuesday.

- He watches TV every night.

- She always buys ice cream after school.

Answer

Ayu always drinks milk before she goes to school.

31. tanya jawab falam bahasa inggris 10 soal tentang go to school​


Jawaban:

10 pertanyaan dan 10 jawaban dalam bahasa Inggris beserta artinya:

1. Q: How are you? (Apa kabar?)

A: I am great as usual. (Saya baik-baik saja seperti biasanya)

2. Q: Where does your uncle from? (Dari mana asal pamanmu?)

A: He is from Kebumen, Central Java. (Dia berasal dari Kebumen, Jawa Tengah)

3. Q: How do you go to school? (Bagaimana kamu pergi ke sekolah?)

A: I go to school by bicycle. (Saya pergi ke sekolah mengendarai sepeda)

4. Q: How often you visit this place? (Seberapa sering kamu mengunjungi tempat ini?)

A: I visit this place twice a week. (Saya mengunjungi tempat ini dua kali seminggu.)

5. Q: What is your father? (Apa pekerjaan ayahmu?)

A: My father is a policeman. (Ayahku adalah seorang polisi)

6. Q: What do you usually do in the morning? (Apa yang biasanya kamu lakukan di pagi hari?)

A: I usually make my bed and then take a bath in the morning. (Saya biasanya merapikan tempat tidur lalu mandi di pagi hari)

7. Q: Why does Shinta come late? (Kenapa Shinta datang terlambat?)

A: She gets a traffic jam. (Dia terjebak macet)

8. Q: When does your brother go to US? (Kapan saudara laki-lakimu pergi ke Amerika?)

A: He goes to US this afternoon. (Dia pergi ke Amerika siang ini)

9. Q: Where does Anton live? (Dimana Anton tinggal?)

A: Anton lives at Jalan Pramuka number 5 Semarang. (Anton tinggal di jalan pramuka nomor 5 Semarang)

10. Q: What is your favourite song? (Apa lagu favoritmu?)

A: My favourite song is 'A whole new world'. (Lagu favoritku adalah "A whole new world")

Pembahasan

Simple Present Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung sehari-hari (Daily routines).

Kata keterangan yang sering digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

• Every day

• Every week

• every month

• every year

• once a week

• twice a day

• three times a month

• three times a year

• always

• often

• usually

• seldom

• rarely

• dan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

(+) S + V1 atau S + V1s/es

(-) S + do/ does + V1

(?) Do/ Does + S + V1?

NB:

V1 dan Do untuk subject I, You, They, We

V1s/es dan Does untuk subject He, She, It
