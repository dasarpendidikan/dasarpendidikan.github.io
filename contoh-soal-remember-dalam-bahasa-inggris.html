6. Bahasa InggrisChange this text into Passive Voice-Who told you that it was not their main duty to remember to shut down the computer before they close the office.​


Jawaban:Change into Passive Voice

1. Active : Everybody loves Mr. Brown

Passive : Mr. Brown is loved by everyone

Pola Passive Simple Present Tense :

Active : S + V1/V-s/V-es + O

Passive : O + AM/IS/ARE + V3 + S

AM digunakan untuk subject I

IS digunakan untuk subject HE, SHE, IT, dan SINGULAR NOUN (kata benda tunggal)

ARE digunakan untuk subject THEY, WE, YOU, dan PLURAL NOUN (kata benda jamak)

AM, IS, dan ARE disesuaikan dengan posisi object yang berubah posisi menjadi subject

2. Active : They are building a new stadium near the station

Passive : A new stadium near the station are being built by them

Pola Passive Simple Continuous Tense :

Active : S + AM/IS/ARE + V-ing + O

Passive : O + AM/IS/ARE + BEING + V3 + S

3. Active : The wolf ate the princess

Passive : The princess was eaten by the wolf

Pola Passive Simple Past Tense :

Active : S + V2 + O

Passive : O + WAS/WERE + V3 + S

WAS digunakan untuk subject I, HE, SHE, IT, dan SINGULAR NOUN (kata benda tunggal)

WERE digunakan untuk subject THEY, WE, YOU, dan PLURAL NOUN (kata benda jamak)

WAS dan WERE disesuaikan dengan posisi object yang berubah posisi menjadi subject

4. Active : At six o'clock someone wass telling a story

Passive : A story was being told by someone at six o'clock

Pola Passive Past Continuous Tense :

Active : S + WAS/WERE + V-ing + O

Passive : O + WAS/WERE + BEING +V3 + S

5. Active : Somebody has drunk all the milk

Passive : All the milk has been drunk by someone

Pola Passive Present Perfect Tense :

Active : S + HAVE/HAS + V3 + O

Passive : O + HAVE/HAS + BEEN + V3 + O

HAS digunakan untuk subject HE, SHE, IT dan SINGULAR NOUN (kata benda tunggal)

HAVE digunakan untuk THEY, WE, I, YOU, dan PLURAL NOUN (kata benda jamak)

HAVE dan HAS disesuaikan dengan posisi object yang berubah posisi menjadi subject

6. Active : I had cleaned all the windows before the storm

Passive : All the windows had been cleaned by me before the storm

Pola Passive Past Perfect Tense :

Active : S + HAD + V3 + O

Passive : O + HAD + BEEN + V3 + S

7. Active : A workman will repair the computer tomorrow

Passive : The computer will be repaired by the workman tomorrow

Pola Passive Simple Future Tense :

Active : S + WILL + V1 + O

Passive : O + WILL + BE + V3 + S

8. Active : By next year the students will have studied passive

Passive : Passive will have been studied by the students by next year

Pola Passive Future Perfect Tense :

Active : S + WILL HAVE + V3 + O

Passive : O + WILL HAVE BEEN + V3 + S

9. Active : James might cook dinner

Passive : Dinner might be cooked by James

Pola Passive Present Modals :

Active : S + PRESENT MODALS + V1 + O

Passive : O + PRESENT MODALS + BE + V1 + S

Contoh Present Modal adalah CAN, COULD, MAY, MIGHT, WILL, WOULD, MUST, MAY, MIGHT, SHALL, SHOULD, dan OUGHT TO

10. Active : Somebody must have taken my wallet

Passive : My wallet must have been taken by somebody

Pola Passive Past Modals :

Active : S + PAST MODALS + V3 + O

Passive : O + PAST MODALS + BEEN + V3 + S

Contoh Past Modals adalah SHOULD HAVE, MUST HAVE, MAY HAVE, MIGHT HAVE dan OUGHT TO HAVE

20. (+) i am remember(-) (?) am i remember ?​


(+) i am remember

(-) i am not remember

(?) am i remember ?

penjelasan :

kalau kalimatnya negatif di isikan not

Penjelasan:

» Pengertian Simple Present Tense

Simple present tense adalah tenses (pola kalimat) yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kebiasaan (habitual action), kebenaran umum (Fakta), atau kejadian yang terjadi pada saat ini. Bentuk kata kerja ini paling sering digunakan dalam bahasa Inggris.

» Rumus Simple Present Tense

(+) Subjek (I, You, We, They) + V1 + O

(+) Subjek (She, He, It) + V1 + s/es + O

(?) do + Subjek (I, You, We, They) + V1

(?) do + Subjek (I, You, We, They) + V1(?) does + Subjek (She, He, It) + V1

(?) do + Subjek (I, You, We, They) + V1

(?) do + Subjek (I, You, We, They) + V1(?) does + Subjek (She, He, It) + V1

» Rumus Nominal Simple Present Tense(tanpa

kalimat kata kerja)

(+) S + be (Am, Is, Are) + Adj/Noun/Adverb

(-) S + be (Am, Is, Are) + not + Adj/Noun/Adverb

(?) be (Am, Is, Are) + S + Adj/Noun/Adverb

Kalimat tersebut tidak mengandung keterangan waktu, sehingga dapat diltulis dalam Simple Present Tense atau Simple Past Tense.

Penggunaan kata not menunjukkan bahwa kalimat tersebut adalah kalimat negatif.

Simple Present Tense digunakan untuk menunjukkan sebuah fakta.

Formula penulisan kalimat negatif tense ini adalah S + do/does + not + V1.

Berikut adalah aturan Subject-Verb Agreement tentang penggunaan do/does:

Subjek James memiliki kata ganti he, maka kata kerja bantu yang dibutuhkan adalah does dan kalimatnya menjadi "James does not remember me".

Simple Past Tense digunakan untuk menunjukkan sebuah peristiwa yang terjadi di waktu lampau atau sudah lewat.

Formula penulisan kalimat negatif tense ini adalah S + did + not + V1.

Berdasarkan pola Simple Past Tense tersebut didapatkan kalimat "James did not remember me".

Dengan demikian, jawaban yang benar adalah "James does not remember me".
